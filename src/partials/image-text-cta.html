Have you been reading our Field Note entries this year? If you have, you’ve learned about researchers like Tim Assal, who’s using the CI-110 Plant Canopy Imager to help predict forest response to drought, and Simone Whitecloud who has used our CI-340 Handheld Photosynthesis System to study how alpine plants adapt to environmental stress. We are so thankful for the scientists who are using our tools to do important work, and for their willingness to share that work with our CID community.
